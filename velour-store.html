Frequently Asked
How long does shipping take?+
We dispatch within 24–48 hours. Standard delivery 3–5 business days across India. Express 1–2 day delivery available at checkout. Tracking link sent via SMS & email once dispatched.
Are products safe for sensitive skin?+
Yes — all VELOUR products are dermatologist tested and gentle on sensitive skin. Free from parabens, sulfates, artificial fragrance, and common irritants. Always recommend a patch test before first use.
What is your return policy?+
30-day hassle-free returns. Not 100% satisfied? Contact our support team and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange — no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.
How do I track my order?+
Once your order ships, you'll receive email and SMS with your tracking number. Visit our Track Order page and enter your order number for real-time delivery updates.
Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?+
Absolutely. VELOUR is 100% cruelty-free and certified vegan. We never test on animals and no ingredients are derived from animal sources. Proud members of PETA's Beauty Without Bunnies.
Do you offer discounts or subscriptions?+
Yes! New customers get 10% off with code VELOUR10. Our Subscribe & Save gives 15% off every recurring order. We also run seasonal sales and exclusive email subscriber deals.
Can I use multiple products together?+
Our range is designed to work harmoniously together. Start with Cleanser, followed by Serum, finish with Face Oil. Our team can help build a personalised routine for your skin type.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, EMI on select banks, and Cash on Delivery across most pin codes in India.
